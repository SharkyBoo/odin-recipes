Ipoh shredded Chicken Hor Fun

Description:

Ipoh is know for its high-quality water.
Ipoh hor fun is made with unfiltered spring water that supposedly really boosts the quality of the ultra smooth and silky texture of hor fun.
It is almost like eating chee cheong fun.
